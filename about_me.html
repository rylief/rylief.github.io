Hi, I'm Rylie Foster. I have a B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science from Reed College, and
I'm interested in machine learning, equation-free modeling, and the ethical and safe development
of data science and AI tools.

I'm especially interested in applying tools from data-driven dynamical systems to ecological data,
and developing explainable models for the interactions between climate and biodiversity in
specific ecological systems.
